I'm a geoscience researcher at **IISER Kolkata**, where I sit at the crossroads of **planetary physics**, **data science**, and **computational engineering**. My work is driven by a single conviction: that the next breakthrough in understanding Earth's hidden architecture will not come from one discipline — it will come from building **bridges between them**.

From modeling subsurface gas hydrate deposits with CSIR-NGRI to training ML models that read geological formations through well-log patterns, I design systems where **scientific rigor meets software craftsmanship**.

I don't just study the Earth — I build the tools to interrogate it.

## `⬡` Featured Projects

> *Each project here is a chapter in a larger story — one where geoscience meets computation, and data becomes discovery.*

---

### 🔴 `[Research]` Gas Hydrate Exploration — CSIR-NGRI
> **Collaborative Research · National Geophysical Research Institute**

Conducted seismic and geochemical analysis to identify and characterize gas hydrate deposits in offshore sedimentary basins. Integrated multi-source geological datasets to map prospective zones, contributing to India's national energy resource mapping initiative.

`Python` `Seismic Analysis` `GIS` `Subsurface Modeling`

---

### 🟡 `[ML]` Lithology Prediction via Well Log Analysis
> **ML-Driven Formation Evaluation**

Designed and trained a machine learning classification pipeline to predict lithological formations from well-log data (GR, Resistivity, Density, Neutron). Achieved high-accuracy stratigraphic interpretation, automating a process traditionally requiring expert manual analysis.

`Python` `scikit-learn` `Pandas` `Feature Engineering` `Classification`

---

### 🟠 `[Climate AI]` IMD Heatwave Prediction & Weather Intelligence
> **Disaster Prediction & Climate Analysis**

Built a predictive analytics system for heatwave forecasting using India Meteorological Department datasets. Combined time-series modeling with spatial analysis to generate early-warning indicators for extreme thermal events across Indian geography.

`Python` `Time-Series` `Remote Sensing` `IMD Data` `Climate Modeling`

---

### 🔵 `[Active Research]` Seismic Tomography — Earth Structure Imaging
> **Ongoing · IISER Kolkata**

Developing computational workflows for 3D subsurface velocity modeling using seismic waveform inversion. Targets deep crustal and mantle structure characterization — aiming to resolve tectonic anomalies beneath complex geological terrains.

`FORTRAN` `SAC` `MATLAB` `Python` `IRIS` `Waveform Inversion`

---

### 🟣 `[AI / NLP]` Hybrid RAG Model with Live Web Intelligence
> **Retrieval-Augmented Generation · Real-Time Data**

Engineered a hybrid Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipeline that fuses static knowledge bases with dynamic web-scraped data streams. Designed for scientific query answering and knowledge synthesis — built for researchers who need answers at the speed of thought.

`Python` `LangChain` `FAISS` `Web Scraping` `LLM Integration` `RAG`

---

### 🟢 `[Web App]` File Forge — Video + Caption Generator
> **Full-Stack Creative Tool**

A web application that automates video generation and intelligent caption creation from uploaded content. Built with a clean, responsive interface and backend pipeline optimized for media processing workflows.

`Python` `Web Dev` `FFmpeg` `AI Captioning` `File Processing`

---

### ⚪ `[Automation]` Telegram Quiz Bot
> **Educational Automation**

Developed an interactive Telegram bot that delivers timed quizzes, tracks scores, and manages leaderboards. Deployed for academic communities — making knowledge testing lightweight, accessible, and engaging.

`Python` `python-telegram-bot` `SQLite` `Bot API`

---

### ⚫ `[Automation]` Telegram Moderation Bot
> **Community Intelligence**

Built an AI-assisted moderation bot for Telegram groups — capable of detecting spam, filtering content, managing user permissions, and logging moderation events. Designed for scale across active research and academic communities.

`Python` `NLP` `Telegram API` `Moderation Logic`

---

### 🔷 `[Data Engineering]` Automated Seismology Pipeline — IRIS
> **Scientific Infrastructure**

Engineered a fully automated data ingestion, preprocessing, and archival pipeline for seismological waveform data from the IRIS (Incorporated Research Institutions for Seismology) network. Streamlines large-scale seismic data workflows that previously required hours of manual effort.

`Python` `ObsPy` `IRIS/FDSN API` `Automation` `Data Pipeline`
